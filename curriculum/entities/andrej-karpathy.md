## Overview
Former VP of AI at Tesla, former OpenAI research scientist, and one of the most influential AI educators in the world. Known for exceptional ability to explain complex AI concepts from first principles. Creator of the LLM Wiki pattern that this vault is built on.

## Key Contributions / Role
- **LLM Wiki Pattern**: Proposed the persistent, compounding wiki approach to personal knowledge management using LLMs — the foundational idea behind this vault.
- **Neural Networks from scratch**: Famous series of tutorials (micrograd, makemore, nanoGPT) teaching neural networks from scratch in Python.
- **"Software 2.0"**: Coined the term describing neural networks as a new programming paradigm.
- **Tesla Autopilot**: Led the AI/vision team building autonomous driving systems.
- **OpenAI**: Early researcher, helped shape direction of early GPT work.
