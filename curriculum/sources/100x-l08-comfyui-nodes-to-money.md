## Summary

Lecture 8 is the bridge lecture — "technical to commercial." After three weeks of deep ComfyUI engine room work, Sridev reframes everything learned as a commercial toolkit. The session had no new workflow building; it was explicitly a product-thinking and market-positioning lecture. Title: "Nodes to Money." Sridev acknowledged feedback ratings had dipped and called L06 (fine-tuning) the hardest technical content in the module — everything from here gets easier.

The core insight: ComfyUI is not an image generator. It is an AI pipeline engine. The 10 products listed are starting points, not a ceiling.

## Key Ideas

- **ComfyUI = pipeline engine, not image generator**: "Photoshop vs Canva" analogy. Canva users are designers; ComfyUI users are engineers building tools on top of an engine.
- **Node-based skill is transferable**: Learning Comfy teaches node-based thinking. FreePik Spaces, n8n, and all workflow tools follow the same paradigm. Learn one, the rest get easier.
- **ComfyUI vs Proprietary scorecard**: ComfyUI wins 9 categories; proprietary wins 6. ComfyUI wins on: control, character/brand consistency, fine-tuning, reproducibility, creative flexibility, cost at scale, IP ownership, product integration, dependency risk. Proprietary wins on: out-of-the-box quality, speed, ease of use, scalability, safety/compliance, on-device use.
- **Decision framework**: Proprietary for speed/simplicity/consumer apps. ComfyUI for control/consistency/custom training/real products.
- **10 products buildable now**: (1) Interior design restyling tool, (2) AI apparel try-on engine, (3) Product photoshoot engine, (4) Brand style generator, (5) AI avatar/influencer engine, (6) Architecture/real estate visual generator, (7) Fashion lookbook generator, (8) E-commerce product variation engine, (9) Character-consistent illustration book, (10) Social media content factory.
- **Biggest opportunity right now**: AI films — ad films, brand films, short films. Space is ripe; micro-agencies forming; $40-50/month in subscriptions replaces millions in production budget. Recruiters from Ogilvy actively hiring.
- **ComfyUI's place in filmmaking**: Open-source video models (WAN, LTX) are behind CDance/VO/Kling for creative quality. ComfyUI shines for image consistency work within those pipelines.
- **CDance 2.0**: Sridev's pick for best video model. Up to 9 inputs (image + audio + text). Available on Higgsfield and OpenArt.
- **Text in images**: Proprietary always wins. Use NanaBanana for text rendering.
- **Sridev's own YouTube pipeline**: AI-generated — voice cloned, avatar AI, human editors handle motion design on long-form.
